#Q1 Delicious Spicy Chicken Pasta

Delicious Spicy Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 250g penne pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Grated parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. Return cooked chicken to the skillet. Stir in diced tomatoes, paprika, cayenne pepper, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until well combined with the chicken and sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated parmesan cheese.
